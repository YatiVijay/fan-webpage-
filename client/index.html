Shruti Sinha was born on 28 March 1998 in Delhi, India. She attended Somerville school, Delhi. She was fond of dancing since childhood and has learned belly dancing, hip-hop dance, jazz, and contemporary dance for 8 yearse. She has also received training in classical dance for 10 yaers. She belongs to a Hindu Kayastha Family. She is the daughter of Late Anil Kumar Sinha and Meeta Sinha. Her father died due to heart failure. She has an elder sister Akruti Sinha and a cute dog Chulbul.
